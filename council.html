Meet Our Dedicated Council Members

IT Coordinators

The Information and Technology Coordinator will manage and maintain the inventory of unassigned laptops, oversee random reallocation every 45 days, and ensure course-specific requirements are met. They will also handle laptop repairs, resolve internet issues, and document decisions through the appropriate channels.

Discipline Coordinators

The Discipline Coordinator will enforce campus rules, ensuring students adhere to schedules, attend classes punctually, and maintain discipline during external visits. They will also address misconduct, reallocate rooms every 45 days, and oversee campus elections, ensuring a smooth, organized environment.

Health and hygiene coordinators

The Health & Hygiene Coordinator will ensure students' well-being by maintaining access to common medicines and promoting fitness on campus. They will also coordinate transportation in case of severe medical emergencies.

Outreach coordinator's

The Outreach Coordinator will manage and organize digital content, including photos and videos, for various activities, ensuring timely communication between the center, volunteers, and community members. They will also create engaging social media posts, coordinate with the social media team, and maintain a campus-specific blog to boost online presence.

(T&P) Training and placement coordinator

The Training and Placement Coordinator will assist students in applying for internships and jobs, and correspond with companies for recruitment. They will organize interview readiness sessions and coordinate all training and placement activities, ensuring positive company-student interactions.

Culture coordinator

The Cultural Coordinator will organize festival celebrations and create long-term clubs or interest groups for students to engage in. They will also plan events that foster student participation in various cultural and recreational activities.

Food coordinators

The Food Coordinator will assign kitchen and cleaning duties impartially, create meal schedules, and ensure food is served during designated times. They will also plan menus, organize grocery purchases, and resolve any food-related grievances raised by members.

Academic Coaches

Mentored 34 students to support their academic progress and personal development. Offering individualized support to students, addressing their specific challenges and goals. Collaborated with peers to exchange best practices and improve overall student support services.

English Coordinators

The English Coordinator will organize language classes, activities, and provide personalized support to enhance students' English proficiency. They will also track progress, promote campus-wide English communication.

Independent committee

Organized transparent council elections, led community meetings, and managed student conflicts, ensuring fair outcomes. Managed data, arranged rooms for 100 students every 45 days, and created recreational clubs and community events. Collaborated with council on campus operations, supported student well-being, and assisted with the selection of the new Independent Committee.
